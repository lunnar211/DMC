Damak Multiple Campus

Damak Multiple Campus is a QAA Accredited affiliate Campus of Tribhuvan University. It lies in Damak, Jhapa, Nepal supported and managed by the community. With affiliation from HSEB Damak multiple campus offers 10+2 program in Science, Management, Humanities and Education. It was established in 2038 BS
